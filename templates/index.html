About Us

The PGA Swindle was created by a group of Letchworth golf club members in 2015. The group meets every Saturday morning at 8:30. We run a competition every week based on individual Stableford scores and we also run 2 seasonal competitions, a winter league and a summer league. The leagues take the scores from each week and add them to a league table. The group includes member of handicap levels from 5 to 25 and runs its own parallel handicap system based on performance in the league to ensure fair distribution of prize money.
