PGA Society – Letchworth Golf Club
Rules for Winter 2018 Season
1 Season
1a The PGA 2018 Winter season will start on Saturday 20th October 2018.
1b The season will run for 24 consecutive weeks (20th Oct 2018 – 30th Mar 2019).
1c Each round will take place on a Saturday morning with the first tee-off time being at 8.30am. Scores obtained by players outside of this schedule will not count towards the league or swindle.
2 Entry, Fees & Swindle
2a Entry fee for season is £10 per player (non-refundable).
2b Anyone may play along on a Saturday morning at any time during the season, but their scores will not count towards the league until after their entry fee has been received by the committee.
2c League players are required to pay a 20p fee for each “blob” during a round, up to a maximum of £1.
2d Only players who have paid their league entry fee may participate in the swindle.
2e Swindle fees are £4 per round per player.
2f Swindle prizes are as follows:
a) If there are 10 players or less…
Winner receives two thirds of the swindle pot (rounded off to the nearest £).
Runner up receives one third of the swindle pot (rounded off to the nearest £).
b) If there are 11 players or more…
Winner receives one half of the swindle pot (rounded off to the nearest £).
Best front nine score receives one quarter of the swindle pot (rounded off to the nearest £).
Best back nine score receives one quarter of the swindle pot (rounded off to the nearest £).
Please note: No individual player can receive more than one prize. If this is the case, then the next best front nine or back nine will win the prize.
3 Scoring System
3a Points will be awarded to all players for each round. A players’ stableford score will be the points they receive towards their league total.
3b Only one joker per player per season is allowed. Players must announce their intention to play their joker to the whole group and before they tee-off.
3c Everyone playing a joker will receive a 50% bonus to their score for the day. (Bonuses will be rounded up to the nearest whole number).
3d Points will only be awarded if there are 4 players or more participating in each individual week.
3e Joker can be played at any round. There is no minimum player participation requirement (other than rule 3d).
4 Handicaps
4a All players will start the season using their official club handicap. (e.g. official h/cap = 12.2c therefore PGA h/cap will be 12.2c (12)).
4b If a player does not have an official handicap, the committee will allocate them a starting PGA handicap.
4c Hanidicap changes will be made throughout the season using the following method:
a) If a player scores 29 points or less, their handicap will increase by 0.3
(e.g.1 – Player h/cap = 12.2c (12): Scores 28 points: New h/cap = 12.5c (13).
(e.g.2 – Player h/cap = 12.2c (12): Scores 24 points: New h/cap = 12.5c (13).
b) If a player scores 38 points they will be cut by 1 shot. For every point over 38 there will be an additional 0.2 cut. (i.e 38 = 1 cut, 39 = 1.2 cut, 40 = 1.4 cut, etc.).
(e.g.1 – Player h/cap = 12.2c (12): Scores 39 points: New h/cap = 11.0c (11).
(e.g.2 – Player h/cap = 12.2c (12): Scores 43 points: New h/cap = 10.2c (10).
Please note: Each player’s PGA handicap can now fluctuate up and down freely and will no longer be influenced mid-season by their official club handicap (other than the fact that their PGA handicap is set by their official handicap at the start of the season, as per rule 4a).
5 League Table
5a All players points will be added to the league table each week. Each player’s points total will comprise of their best eight results throughout the season.
5b There will no longer be a season final playoff. (See rule 8b).
6 General Play
6a Round scores will be calculated using the Stableford scoring system.
6b No gimmies. Players must putt out.
7 Prizes
7a The prize fund is dependant on the number of paid entries for the season.
7b The top 5 point scorers in the league, at the end of the season, will receive prizes. Ammounts will be anounced towards the end of the league season.
8 Committee
8a This season’s committee members are Kamal, Julien, Ringo & Joe
8b The committee will be responsible for keeping league players up to date with league tables, handicap changes and any other news on a weekly basis via email and our WhatsApp group.
